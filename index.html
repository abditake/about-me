About Abdinasir
Author: Abdinasir Yussuf Project: About-me

Welcome to my Website called about-me. My name is Abdinasir Yussuf. I was born in Nairobi Kenya and was raised in America. I moved to America when I was around 3 months old. Most of my childhood I lived in the Mid-west(Ohio, Minnesota, and Kansas). We would move frequently since my mom needed to find work and move closer to family from time to time. I went to Seattle Central College for my AA DTA. I planned on transferring pre-med around 2019 but that's when covid really started going. The past two years I've just been working and taking care of my family. I had a lot of time to think about what I wanted to do. I still plan on going into the medical field because my dream is becoming a Pediatrician; however this year it really hit me that I need the financial stability to make sure my family is taken care of.
